# Meet the Drone

## The Hook
Flo breaks the fourth wall for the first time — turns to the drone and introduces it.

## The Line
"You've been asking how I film everything. Meet my drone. I built it so I never have to think about a camera. And my mom can watch live so she always knows I'm okay."

## Why It Works
- That last line tells you everything about Flo in one sentence: adventurous enough to live in a van in the desert, thoughtful enough to make sure her mom doesn't worry
- Breaks the fourth wall — she's been filmed this whole time and finally acknowledges it
- Relatable to every demographic: young people who left home, parents watching kids wander, anyone who ever worried about someone they love
- Sets up the drone as a character the audience now has a relationship with
- Natural "how I film" content that every creator channel eventually does, but Flo's version has soul

## Technical Notes
- This should feel like drone footage where Flo turns and looks directly at camera
- The drone should wobble/react when she acknowledges it (personality moment)
- Desert or van setting — somewhere that makes the "mom watching" line hit harder
- This is the ONE video where the audience learns the camera is a drone. After this, it's just how things are filmed.

## When to Release
Could be the first video, or after a few posts where people are already watching Flo's content and wondering about the camera perspective.
