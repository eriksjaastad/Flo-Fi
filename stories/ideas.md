# Story Ideas

One-liners and rough concepts. When one gets fleshed out, it becomes its own file.

## The Van Build (series)
Could be a multi-part series — "Building Your Van" — or sprinkled across other content.
- **First cut** — Cutting the ceiling fan hole. Flo bought a brand new van and now she's cutting holes in it. The drone watches. Flo stares at the jigsaw. The van stares back. Terrifying.
  - The moment of no return — measuring three times, hand shaking, drill goes in
  - Drone probably backs up instinctively
- **The electrical nightmare** — Nobody knows how to do electrical until they do it. Flo watching YouTube tutorials at 2am. Wires everywhere. Circuit breaker pops. Drone dims sympathetically.
  - Flo with wire strippers, looking confused
  - The triumphant moment when the lights actually turn on
- **The packing problem** — Where does everything go? Overpacking, underpacking, reorganizing five times.
  - Everything laid out on the ground next to the van
  - Realizing half of it doesn't fit
  - The final minimalist version that actually works
- **The aesthetic** — Her van is girly but budget. She's not rich. Think creative solutions, thrift store finds, DIY curtains, fairy lights doing heavy lifting. Cozy, not Pinterest-perfect.
- First night sleeping in the van
- Installing solar panels on the roof (drone gets a great overhead angle)
- The water system — figuring out where to put the tank, first time the pump works
- Test drive with everything rattling
