# The Daisy Collaboration

## The Hook
Two AI van life characters meet on the road. The first-ever AI character crossover video.

## The Scene
Somewhere in the Southwest. Daisy pulls up, Flo's drone buzzes around being nosy, they just vibe. No drama. Two characters who exist in the same world having a moment.

## Why It Works
- Two creators collaborating through their AI characters is something nobody has done yet — landmark moment for AI character content
- Cross-pollination blows up both channels
- The story writes itself: two characters on the road, meeting naturally
- Shows respect for the creator who inspired us

## The Setup
1. First video credits Daisy's creator as inspiration — honest, generous, opens the door
2. After first video drops, reach out to Daisy's creator: show them Flo, show them the pipeline, pitch the collab
3. Worst case: they say no. Best case: biggest video for both channels.

## Technical Approach
- Grok Aurora multi-image API can stitch characters together in the same scene
- Feed Flo images + Daisy-style images into the same storyboard call
- Would need Daisy's creator to provide or approve their character's appearance
- Could also be done as split-screen / reaction style if same-scene is too complex

## When to Release
NOT the first video. Flo needs to exist on her own first. This is a milestone collab after the channel has some traction.
