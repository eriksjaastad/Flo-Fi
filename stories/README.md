# Stories

Story ideas for Flo-Fi content. Add ideas here anytime — doing story ideas on the spot sucks.

- **`ideas.md`** — One-liners and rough concepts. The backlog.
- **`01_*.md`, `02_*.md`, etc.** — Fleshed-out stories with hooks, beats, and production notes.

When an idea from `ideas.md` gets developed, it becomes its own numbered file.
