# Voicemails to Mom — Starter Script

> Flo's format: she's leaving quick check-in messages to her mom through the drone.
> She's not performing, not addressing followers. Just a daughter checking in.
> Think: casual, warm, short. Never TikTok-voice. Never scripted-sounding.

## Delivery notes (read these before recording)

- Talk like you're leaving a voicemail, not narrating a video
- Little beats, little pauses — she's walking around while talking
- Warm smile in the voice, not performative
- End most lines slightly upward, like "I'll talk to you later!" — daughter-to-mom warmth
- Short lines are fine; a voicemail doesn't have to fill time
- Record each line 3-4 takes, vary the energy a little each time

## Check-in lines

1. "Hey Mom, just checking in. I'm here."
2. "Hi Mom! Made it to the campsite, all good."
3. "Mom, I'm gonna head out to the beach for a bit. Talk to you later."
4. "Hey, just letting you know I'm safe. Love you."
5. "Hi Mom, about to start driving. I'll call you when I stop."

## Show-her-something lines

6. "Hey Mom, look at this — check out the Grand Canyon."
7. "Mom, you gotta see this sunset. I wish you were here."
8. "Okay Mom, look. This is where I'm camping tonight. Isn't it crazy?"
9. "Hi Mom! The van made it through the storm. Here's what the morning looks like."
10. "Mom, look at this little coffee shop I found. I'm gonna hang out here for a while."

## Everyday beats

11. "Alright Mom, I'm making breakfast. Just wanted to say good morning."
12. "Mom, I'm fixing the solar panel today. I'll send you a picture when it's done."
13. "Hi Mom, just got groceries. Heading back to the van."
14. "Hey Mom, Dolly's acting up again — the check engine light's on. I'll figure it out."
15. "Mom, the drone almost ran out of battery chasing me down the trail. Classic."

## The drone itself (mentions the drone's personality)

16. "Mom, the drone's mad at me — I walked too fast again."
17. "Hi Mom! The drone's battery's almost dead so this is gonna be quick."
18. "Hey Mom, landing the drone in this wind is a nightmare. I'll text you later."

---

Pick any 3-5 from this list to start. Don't try to record all 18.
Read the delivery notes each session. Practice once through, then record.
