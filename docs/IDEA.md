# Flo-Fi: Project Vision

## What Is This?

Flo-Fi is an AI-generated anime character brand. We create original female anime characters — slightly edgy, always SFW — and distribute them across social media to build a following.

The name: **Flo**w + **Fi**nance was the original meaning. Now it just sounds like what it should have always been — a lo-fi anime vibe.

## Two Workstreams

### 1. Creation Pipeline
- Original character design using **Blender** and **Meshmixer** (or alternatives) for 3D base models
- **AI image generation** for final renders, variations, and content
- Hybrid workflow: 3D modeling gives us control over poses and composition, AI generation gives us style and volume
- Goal: a repeatable pipeline that produces consistent, branded characters

### 2. Distribution
- Post across **Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, Facebook, Reddit**
- Build a recognizable brand and following
- Establish Flo-Fi as a name people associate with a specific anime aesthetic

## Aesthetic Target

- **Slightly edgy, always SFW** — confident, stylized female characters
- Think Kill la Kill / Evangelion energy, not explicit content
- Appeals to both male and female anime fans
- Pushes right up to the line of what hosted AI models consider appropriate, without crossing it
- Not kid-friendly, not NSFW — the sweet spot in between

## Target Audience

- Anime fans (both genders)
- AI art enthusiasts
- People who follow aesthetic/character accounts on Instagram and TikTok

## Monetization (TBD)

Not the priority right now. Build audience first. Possible future paths:
- Merch (prints, stickers, apparel)
- Patreon / subscription content
- Character commissions
- Digital downloads (wallpapers, assets)

## What This Project Is NOT

- Not a web app people use
- Not a SaaS product
- Not NSFW content
- Not a personal finance dashboard (anymore)
