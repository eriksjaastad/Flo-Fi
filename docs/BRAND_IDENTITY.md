# Flo-Fi: Brand Identity (Draft)

## Name

**Flo-Fi** — evokes lo-fi beats, chill anime vibes, flow state. Short, memorable, brandable.

## Brand Essence

AI-generated anime characters with attitude. Not your typical cute-only anime — Flo-Fi characters have edge, confidence, and personality.

## Aesthetic Direction

### Tone
- **Confident, not aggressive** — characters look like they own the room
- **Stylish, not provocative** — fashion-forward, bold outfits, but always SFW
- **Cool, not cold** — there's warmth and personality behind the edge

### Visual Style (To Be Refined)
- Anime-influenced but not strictly traditional anime
- Rich color palettes — neons, deep purples, teals, warm oranges
- Atmospheric lighting — think golden hour, neon cityscapes, moody interiors
- Mix of 3D-rendered and AI-generated aesthetics

### Reference Points
- Kill la Kill (confidence, bold design)
- Evangelion (mood, atmosphere)
- Cyberpunk: Edgerunners (color, energy)
- Lo-fi hip hop girl (chill, iconic, brandable)

## Character Guidelines

### What Flo-Fi Characters Are
- Female-presenting (primary focus)
- Distinct personalities expressed through design
- Fashionable — outfits are part of the brand
- Expressive — not blank-faced dolls

### What They're NOT
- Not sexualized / not NSFW
- Not children / not loli
- Not generic — each character should feel like someone

## Voice (Social Media)

- Minimal captions — let the art speak
- When we do caption: casual, slightly mysterious, never try-hard
- Use character names when they exist
- No "generated by AI" apologies — own it

## Content Formats

- **Hero posts**: Single character, high quality, atmospheric
- **Character sheets**: Multiple angles/expressions of one character
- **Series/themes**: Characters grouped by aesthetic (neon, nature, urban, etc.)
- **Short animations**: When pipeline supports it — loops, subtle movement
- **Behind the scenes**: Blender screenshots, process shots (builds trust + engagement)
