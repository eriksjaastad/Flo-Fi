# DIRECTION.md — Flo-Fi

## Goal
Flo-Fi is a fully AI-generated character with her own YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram channels. She's not an influencer — she doesn't address the camera or acknowledge viewers. The format is short video stories where Flo travels around the US leaving video messages for her mom.

## Type: Milestone
Building toward simultaneous channel launch across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. Pipeline (image gen, video, voice, posting) must be fully agentic before launch.

## North Star
Maximum subscribers across all platforms. Flo feels like a real person living her life, not an AI product.

## Current Focus
- Character design and LoRA training for visual consistency
- Video pipeline: image generation → video → voice → lip sync → platform-ready output
- Story development and scene presets

## Future
- Launch on YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram simultaneously
- Expand travel beyond the US
- Grow subscriber base and explore monetization once audience reaches critical mass
